Arnold Schwarzenegger

The man known around the world

Here is a timeline of Arnold Schwarzenegger's accomplishments:

  • 1947 - Born in Thal, Austria
  • 1965 - Serves one year in the Austrian military as a tank driver
  • 1967 - Buys Putzinger Gym
  • 1968 - Moves to United States and starts training supplies company
  • 1970 - Wins Mr. World competition and makes film debut in Hercules in New York
  • 1977 - Features in movie Pumping Iron and releases Education of a Bodybuilder
  • 1979 - Acquires a BS in Business and International Economics
  • 1985 - Becomes naturalized US citizen
  • 2003 - Becomes Governator of California
